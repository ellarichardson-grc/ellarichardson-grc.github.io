At the heart of our pizza-making process is the traditional Woodstone™ method, a time-honored technique that infuses every pie with unparalleled flavor and authenticity. Our wood-fired ovens, crafted to replicate the classic Italian style, reach intense temperatures that cook pizzas to perfection in just minutes. This method creates a crisp, golden crust with a soft, airy interior while imparting a subtle smokiness that only real wood can achieve. Each pizza is hand-stretched and topped with the finest ingredients, ensuring a perfect balance of texture and taste. The Woodstone™ approach is more than a cooking method—it's a tribute to Italian culinary artistry and the timeless tradition of gathering around a beautifully crafted meal.

Nestled in the picturesque Napa Valley, our restaurant blends the essence of Italian tradition with modern innovation to create a truly unique dining experience. Rooted in the art of crafting artisanal pizzas, we take pride in combining age-old techniques with a fresh, contemporary twist. From our hand-stretched dough to our wood-fired ovens, each dish is a harmonious blend of tradition and creativity, designed to delight your senses.

Our menu offers a journey through Italy's culinary heritage with a modern flair. From classic Margherita to bold, innovative creations that highlight seasonal ingredients, we invite you to explore flavors both familiar and unexpected. Pair your meal with a carefully curated selection of Napa Valley wines or Italian imports, chosen to elevate your dining experience.

More than just a restaurant, we aim to be a place where community and connection thrive. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, enjoying a casual evening out, or simply savoring a perfect slice, our doors are always open to welcome you. Here, every meal tells a story—one of tradition, creativity, and the warmth of shared experiences.
